How does life look through the ‘pleasure, passion and purpose’ lens?

Pleasure - Passion - Purpose

I would think that life would be pretty wonderful if work involves the pursuit of pleasure, passion and purpose. I’ve had many jobs in my life and have never achieved all three at once. I’ve had passion and purpose, but to find the pleasure has been difficult. Chasing the rock star high has been elusive to me. I’ve always dreamed of working in an evnironment that engaged creativity and achievement.
