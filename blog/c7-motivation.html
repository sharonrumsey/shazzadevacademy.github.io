How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

"Resilience is the ability to overcome obstacles along the way. Alignment and envisioning help you find out wher you want to go, and rsilience help you get there."

Use of the training on resilience on three levels:

  1. Inner Calm - reaching inside your mind in a way to access mind training to provide optimisim and resilience - finding the path in yourself to achieve no matter what. Achieved by training in mindfulness.
  2. Emotional Resilience - You need to own your own emotion and your own emotional response. In my mind there is a fear of failure and a hope to succeed. Emotional resilience is training to stop your fear learned from past experience stop you from trying. And concentrating on moments of success help and recognizing that the experience is simply that - an experience. We all have failures in our lives and those failures are experiences to be learned from.
  3. Cognitive Resilience - Develops optimism. Even with many experiences in our lives of failure it's important to develop optimisim to continue to achieve and make the effort to try. We need to learn and establish optimism in our thoughts and unlearn pessimism pickedup in our life.

Learning Optimism by:

  1. Be aware of your own strong negative experiential bias. Acknowledge and focus on your successes in life.
  2. Mindfulness - Create objectivity toward your experiences. Your body manifests emotions vividly so your mindfulness needs to review those emotions objectively.
